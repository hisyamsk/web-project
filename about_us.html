About us

Njajan.in is a non-profit website that provide high quality snacks to help and support UMKM (Usaha Mikro Kecil Menengah) advertise and sell their products. We believe UMKM can contribute a significant role to help develop Indonesia's economy to reach its highest point.

Our Products are made from the best, high-quality, all-natural ingredients, to make sure you get the tasty, but also healthy snacks, so you can enjoy them without any worries.
